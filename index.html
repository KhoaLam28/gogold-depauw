About Us

Engineering meets the open road.

GoGold DePauw Racing is a student-run club at DePauw University dedicated to designing, building, and racing a competitive vehicle. From chassis to electronics, every component is a hands-on learning experience.

Design & Engineering

Students across all majors collaborate to design and refine our race vehicle from the ground up each season.

Competition

We compete at collegiate motorsports events, testing our engineering decisions against teams from across the country.

Community

Open to all DePauw students — no prior experience needed. We value curiosity, grit, and a love for building things.

Sponsorship

We partner with companies who want to support the next generation of engineers. Get your logo on our car.
